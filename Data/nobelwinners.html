Nobel Laureates at Project Gutenberg

The Nobel Prize for Literature was first awarded in 1901 to Sully Prudhomme. Since that year it has been awarded every year, other than on six occasions during the First and second World Wars. The list of laureates may be viewed at the official Nobel Site.
