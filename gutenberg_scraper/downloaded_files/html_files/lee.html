Ida LEE (MARRIOTT) (1865-1943)

Read ebooks by Ida Lee

Ida Lee was born at Kelso, New South Wales. She was a Fellow of the Royal Geographical Society (F.R.G.S.) and an Honorary Fellow of the Royal Australian Historical Society (Hon. F.R.A.H.S.). She wrote a number of historical texts, some of which contain previously unpublished material. Publications include:--

The Coming of the British to Australia, 1788-1829 (1906)
Commodore Sir John Hayes (1912)
The Logbooks of the 'Lady Nelson' (1915)
Captain Bligh's Second Voyage to the South Sea (1920)
Early Explorer's in Australia (1925)
